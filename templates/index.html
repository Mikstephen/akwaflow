About AKWAFLOW

Our journey began after an intensive 5-day strategy session with senior stakeholders from NNPC, Nigerian legislators, NOCs, and industry experts, addressing a critical challenge — ensuring accurate custody transfer and calibration of flow meters for oil and gas measurements to improve Nigeria’s revenue assurance.

We are committed to empowering industries through innovative engineering and digital solutions for the precise and efficient flow and measurement of resources — from hydrocarbons and water to data, food, waste, and atmospheric aerosols. Guided by integrity, inclusivity, and first principles thinking, we deliver safe, efficient, and environmentally responsible solutions that create lasting value.

Our vision is to be the world’s trusted partner in flow and environmental intelligence, pioneering precision solutions that move resources and information, reveal atmospheric insights, and drive sustainable progress for communities, industries, and the planet.
